Speak with anyone

Send a voice message to anyone with an email or Facebook message. The recipients don’t need to sign up or install anything to listen or reply. Record for as long as you want and send it to any number of people, pretty simple.
On the web and mobile

Record voice messages and see your inbox on the web or in the Voicegem iOS app. The recievers can listen on their device with no extra software, so no need to worry about what device your friend has.
On your website

Integrate Voicegem into your site to hear from your users, quite literally. Just copy and paste the couple lines of code, and you’re set.
