Problems with Flash?

There is a known Adobe Flash Player issue (occurring in some browsers and most recently the Apple Retina display) that prevents users from clicking Allow on the Settings panel. If you experience this problem, we (1) sincerely apologize, and (2) regret that Adobe has not yet addressed this issue properly.

Solution

There are various workarounds described on the web. We suggest going to the following:

1. Go to the Flash Website Privacy Settings panel

2. Find the entry for www.voicegem.com, and click on Always allow

3. Close the settings tab and refresh Voicegem. You should now be able to record your gem!
